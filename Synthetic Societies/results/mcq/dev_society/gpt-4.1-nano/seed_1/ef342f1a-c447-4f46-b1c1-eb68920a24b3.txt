### **Influence of Oxygen on Decomposition Gases**

  - **Methane** is the predominant gas, especially in wetlands, landfills, and sewage digesters.

- **Oxygen-rich** environments favor CO₂ and water production.  
- **Oxygen-deprived** environments favor methane production.
